## Noise Margin

![[noise_margin.png]]

### Definitions

Measured in $Volts$:

$$
\begin{align}
NM_{H} &= V_{OH} - V_{IH} = \Delta V_{H}\\
NM_{L} &= V_{IL} - V_{OL} \\
\end{align}
$$

### Ideal vs. Real

For a buffer:

$$
\begin{align}
NM_{H} = NM_{L} &= \frac{1}{2} \cdot V_{DD} \\
NM_{H}, NM_{L} &\lt \frac{1}{2} \cdot V_{DD} \\
\end{align}
$$
